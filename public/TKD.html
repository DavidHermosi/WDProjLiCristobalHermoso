Mae Geri (Front Kick)

‚ñº

Mae Geri is the fundamental front snap kick in Karate. The kick is delivered straight forward using the ball of the foot, targeting the opponent's midsection or face. It emphasizes speed, accuracy, and the snap-back motion.

Mawashi Geri (Roundhouse Kick)

‚ñº

Mawashi Geri is a circular kick that travels in an arc toward the target. Using the instep or ball of the foot, this kick can target various heights from low leg to head level. It's one of the most versatile and powerful kicks in Karate.

Ushiro Geri (Back Kick)

‚ñº

Ushiro Geri is a powerful back kick delivered with the heel. The practitioner pivots and drives the heel straight back into the target. This technique generates tremendous power and is effective for creating distance from an opponent.
